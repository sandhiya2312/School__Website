Ro System

Our institution is equipped with a modern Reverse Osmosis (RO) Water Purification System to ensure the supply of clean, safe, and hygienic drinking water for students, staff, and visitors. The RO system is designed to effectively remove impurities, harmful chemicals, heavy metals, bacteria, and dissolved salts from the water, providing pure and safe drinking water throughout the campus.
