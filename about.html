About Us

J.B. Matric. Hr. Sec. School, Kolathur is run by J.B. Educational trust, Kolathur. It was started in 1988 around 1000 students are studying 40 teaching staff amd 26 non-teaching staff are working under the able guidance of the founder Mr.Jayaraman, an excellent teacher awardee and the correspondent Mr.J.Thangavelan B.E.,MBA. There are 12 buses for transport facilities. Well equipped science labs, digital library, smart classes and kids park are also available at the school premises. Special care to each and every student is given by our teachers. Our students are motivated to read and write consequently to present their thoughts in their own sentences. Frequent moral classes are also given to our children. Besides inculcating the talents in the field of curricular activities, extracurricular activities like Sports, Yoga, Karate and dance are given importance.
