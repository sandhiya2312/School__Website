J.B. Matric. Hr. Sec. School, Kolathur is run by J.B. Educational trust, Kolathur.

It was started in 1988. Around 1000 students are studying here. 40 teaching staff and 26 non-teaching staff are working under the able guidance of the founder Mr. Jayaraman, an excellent teacher awardee and the correspondent Mr. J. Thangavelan B.E., MBA.

The school offers well-equipped science labs,smart classes and kids park. We offer special care to every student, motivating them to express their thoughts clearly. Extra-curricular activities like Sports, Yoga, Karate, and dance are given importance. We conduct exhibitions, essay, drawing, and elocution competitions, as well as industrial visits. Parent-teacher meetings are conducted regularly to interact and track student progress. Our motto is to bring out every child as a successful personality.
